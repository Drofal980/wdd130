About Us

We are a small company that specializes in taking people on trips through the cosmos. We have been in business for 10 years and have taken thousands of people on trips through the cosmos. We are a family owned business based in the Milky Way Galaxy. We have a fleet of 10 ships that can take you anywhere you want to go. We have a team of 20 pilots that are highly trained and have been with us for over 5 years, along with teams of engineers and scientists that keep our ships running smoothly. Each ship has a crew of 5 people that will take care of you during your trip. We have a 100% safety record and have never lost a customer. We are fully licensed and insured. We are a member of the Better Business Bureau and have an A+ rating. We are also a member of the International Space Travel Association.

FAQ's

  • How long are the trips?

Our trips can last anywhere from 3 months to 2 years. It all depends on how much you want to see!

  • How much does it cost?

Our trips start at $400,000 at economy, $500,000 for first class, and up

  • Do I need to bring my own spacesuit?

No, we provide all the equipment you will need for your trip.

  • What if I get lost?

Our ships are equipped with a GPS system that will always know where you are. If at any moment you are separated, your spacesuit includes an emergency distress signal
